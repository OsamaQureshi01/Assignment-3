Technological Advancements in Building Construction

The evolution of buildings has always been intertwined with technological advancements. Early structures were limited by the strength of the materials available, but innovations in materials and construction techniques have dramatically expanded what is possible in building design.

  • The Invention of Concrete: The Romans were the first to master concrete, allowing them to build larger and more durable structures. Their use of concrete in arches, vaults, and domes was revolutionary and paved the way for modern construction techniques.
